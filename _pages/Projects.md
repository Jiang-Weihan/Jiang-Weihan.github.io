Research Projects
------

### Rehabilitation robotics and inverse optimal control

Modeling human tracking behavior in rehabilitation settings, with an emphasis on inverse optimal control, system identification, and robust estimation under signal-dependent noise.

### Human motion and behavior modeling

Research experience in identifying motion patterns and interpreting human responses within interactive control systems.

### Precision motion control in industry

Engineering experience in motion algorithm development, prototype debugging, field issue diagnosis, and reliability improvement for precision equipment.

### Robotics competitions and simulation

ROBOCON experience in embodied systems, robot control, simulation, and collaborative engineering.
